SIZE

Here at Pocadetro pizza, we offer differrennt sizes depending on how much you are willing to spend. There are three categories when it comes to piza sizes: small, medium and large. The diferrence is determined by the diameter of the pizza. The pice depends on:

  1. The size of the pizza

  2. The type of crust

  3. The type and number of toppings

  4. How many pizzas you want
